    NETHRAVATHI
    TREK

    The Nethravathi Trek is a beautiful and moderate trekking route located in the Western Ghats of Karnataka. Known for its lush landscapes, serene waterfalls, and the scenic Nethravathi River, this trek offers an unforgettable experience for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The trail takes you through dense forests, rolling hills, and picturesque valleys, providing stunning views of the surrounding landscapes. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced trekker, the Nethravathi Trek is the perfect escape into nature's tranquility.

    KUDHREMUKH
    TREK

    The Kudremukh Trek is a popular and scenic trekking destination located in the Western Ghats of Karnataka. Known for its lush green landscapes, rolling hills, and stunning views, this trek offers an unforgettable adventure for nature lovers and trekking enthusiasts. The trail takes you through dense forests, grasslands, and rugged terrain, leading to the peak of Kudremukh, which resembles the face of a horse, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and mountains. Perfect for both beginner and intermediate trekkers, the Kudremukh Trek is an ideal escape into nature's beauty.

    KODACHADRI
    TREK

    The Kodachadri Trek is a thrilling and scenic adventure located in the Western Ghats of Karnataka. Famous for its lush greenery, rolling hills, and unique mountain shape resembling a horse's face, this trek offers stunning views and a refreshing experience in nature. The trail takes you through dense forests, meadows, and challenging terrain, leading to the Kudremukh peak, where you’re rewarded with panoramic vistas of the surrounding landscape. Ideal for nature enthusiasts and moderate trekkers, the Kudremukh Trek promises an unforgettable journey through one of Karnataka's most beautiful regions.

    BANDAJE FALL
    TREK

    The Bandaje Falls trek is a beautiful hiking trail in Karnataka, India. It takes you through forests and hills to the Bandaje Waterfall, which is about 200 feet high. The trek starts from the village of Nersa and offers a mix of easy and moderate difficulty. The waterfall is most stunning during the monsoon season, making it a great spot for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
